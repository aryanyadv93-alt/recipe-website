Pasta

🕐 Preparation Time: 25 minutes
📊 Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 400g Pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 400ml Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • 4 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Flakes
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Parmesan Cheese for topping

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water according to package instructions.
  2. Drain pasta (reserving 1 cup pasta water) and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add red chili flakes and cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Pour in crushed tomatoes and oregano. Stir well.
  7. Simmer the sauce for 10 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  8. If the sauce is too thick, add some reserved pasta water.
  9. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  10. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
  12. Serve hot while steam is still rising from the dish.
