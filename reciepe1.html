Paneer Butter Masala

🕐 Preparation Time: 30 minutes
📊 Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cut into cubes)
  • 2 medium Tomatoes
  • 1 large Onion
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften completely.
  4. Blend the tomato mixture into a smooth paste using a blender.
  5. Return the paste to the pan and add garam masala and red chili powder.
  6. Cook the sauce for 5 minutes on medium heat.
  7. Add cream and mix well. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Gently add the paneer cubes and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.
