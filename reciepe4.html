Chocolate Cake

🕐 Preparation Time: 50 minutes
📊 Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Hot Water (3/4 cup)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Slowly add hot water to the batter and stir until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  9. Turn the cake out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Once cooled, you can frost with chocolate frosting if desired.
  11. Slice and serve with ice cream or whipped cream.
