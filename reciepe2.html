Veg Biryani

🕐 Preparation Time: 40 minutes
📊 Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables (peas, carrots, potatoes)
  • 1 large Onion (sliced)
  • 4 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • 3-4 Green Chilies
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Biryani Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Mint and Coriander for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Boil water in a large pot with salt and whole spices. Add rice and cook until 70% done. Drain immediately.
  3. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot and fry onions until golden brown. Reserve half for garnish.
  4. Mix yogurt with ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.
  5. In the pot, layer: fried onions, then vegetables mixed with spices.
  6. Spread yogurt mixture over the vegetables.
  7. Top with partially cooked rice and remaining fried onions.
  8. Cover the pot with foil and place a lid on top (dum pukht method).
  9. Cook on high heat for 2 minutes until steam forms, then reduce to low heat.
  10. Cook for 15-20 minutes until rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
  11. Garnish with fresh mint and coriander. Serve hot.
