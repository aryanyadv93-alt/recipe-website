Sandwich

🕐 Preparation Time: 10 minutes
📊 Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Bread
  • 2 tbsp Butter or Mayonnaise
  • 1 Cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 Tomato (sliced)
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 2 slices Cheese
  • Lettuce Leaves
  • Mustard or Sauce
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Take two slices of bread and place them on a clean surface.
  2. Apply butter or mayonnaise evenly on both slices.
  3. Spread mustard or your favorite sauce on one slice.
  4. Layer the lettuce leaves on the buttered slice first.
  5. Add sliced cucumber, tomato, and onion in layers.
  6. Place cheese slices on top of the vegetables.
  7. Sprinkle salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Place the second slice of bread on top to close the sandwich.
  9. If desired, cut the sandwich diagonally to make two triangles.
  10. Serve immediately while fresh and crisp.
